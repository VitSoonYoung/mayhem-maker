Mayhem Maker - LoL ARAM Mayhem Augment Card Maker

A free fan-made tool to create custom ARAM Mayhem augment cards inspired by League of Legends. Choose Silver, Gold, or Prismatic tier, pick an augment icon, write your title and description, and share via a URL or export as a PNG image.

Supported keywords are automatically highlighted in their in-game colors — including Attack Damage, Ability Power, Health, Mana, On-hit, Critical Strike, Armor, Magic Resist, Ability Haste, Life Steal, and more.
